SuiCTF platform

February 10, 2026 1-minute read
CTF • Sui

As a way to learn more about Sui, Move, and vibe coding, I made this CTF platform with Sui Move challenges: https://suictf.com/.

Most challenges are still fairly easy, but I feel like they give a good way to get started in Move and the Sui tools. Note that all challenges were hand-written1, LLMs were only used for the UI. Check it out!

  1. Current models seem to do a terrible job at designing fun CTF challenges. ↩︎
